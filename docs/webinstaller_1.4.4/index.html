Mayhem for Flipper Zero

Mayhem for Flipper Zero -installer

If you have Momentum firmware on your Flipper Zero, then the apps are already on Apps/GPIO. Go to to get precompiled .fap files, or compile your own following this.

To prepare the board for uploading, jumper pin IO0 to GND and press and release reset on the ESP32CAM. Upload the firmware pressing Install button below. For more details on the apps check this video, or take a look of the documentation.

Note 1: If you are using your Flipper to upload the firmware, remember to go to GPIO/USB-UART Bridge. After the upload, reset the ESP32 (or unplug/replug the module).

Note 2: If you do not see the Install button below, your browser might not support this functionality.

Note 3: Unplug the USB-C cable to test the apps.

Enjoy!
